Policies

financing

Our prices are dependent on the place you travel to, the numbers days your stay is, the type of activities you end up doing, etc. There's are our approximate prices dependent on location from our previous clients travel itineraries.

Bali: $80-$250 per person per day

Greece: $70-$420 per person per day

India: $75-$350 per person per day

Dubai: $100-$550 per person per day

Maldives: $90-$360 per person per day

Italy: $95-$450 per person per day

delivery

Since we support businesses that are sustainable, we ourselves try to be as sustainable as possible. Therefore, no paper is used. We email out all of the information needed.

refund policy

There are no refunds on any purchases made. If you can not make it to your trip due to unforeseen circumstances we do try to accomodate you as best as we can with a small fee.
